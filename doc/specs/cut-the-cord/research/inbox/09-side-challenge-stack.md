## Offline-first architecture

The safest architecture is a hard split between an offline core lane and an online prep lane. For the offline core, the organizers themselves recommend Ollama under тАЬOn-Device & Edge AI,тАЭ and Google documents that Gemma can be run with Ollama or llama.cpp on a laptop or other small device without a GPU. For PoliSa, the clean runtime path is: M5Stack and optionally OAK provide local signals; a local process normalizes those signals; a local LLM on the MSI machine does reasoning; a local state manager handles memory and business logic; output goes to a local UI and local audio. If that path works over dead internet, it is aligned with both the published track framing and the judging format. юИАciteюИВturn22view0юИВturn14view11юИВturn13search15юИВturn23view0юИВturn21view0юИБ

The online prep lane should be explicitly non-core. That means Google AI Studio or Gemini for prompt research and evaluation, Replit for collaboration and project scaffolding, and ElevenLabs for optional audio assets or a second-mode extension. A good internal rule is that cloud-produced artifacts may be imported into the demo machine ahead of time, but cloud services must not be required after the demo begins. In practical terms, PoliSa should rehearse with Wi-Fi off, no hotspot, and no hidden fallback calls. If the team wants to show a cloud-enhanced mode at all, it should come after the offline demonstration has already succeeded. юИАciteюИВturn22view0юИВturn22view1юИВturn22view2юИВturn22view3юИВturn21view0юИБ

## M5Stack concepts that strengthen the main track

The most promising M5Stack concept is an **offline scene-and-state explainer**. Core2 provides the touch screen, mic, speaker, and buttons; the camera module provides local visual input; PIR, ToF, light, and ENV sensors provide structured context such as motion, distance, light level, temperature, humidity, and pressure. The host model can then answer questions like тАЬwhat changed,тАЭ тАЬis someone approaching,тАЭ or тАЬwhat should the device do nextтАЭ without sending data off the machine. A second strong concept is a **privacy-first room steward**. The sensors become proof points for why local AI matters: the system sees a person enter, measures proximity, reads lighting and ambient conditions, and explains its decisions locally. The RGB LED and dual button can become explicit trust cues, such as local-only mode, standby mode, or тАЬcloud-enhanced mode disabled.тАЭ That turns privacy and reliability into visible product behavior instead of a slide claim. A third concept is a **handheld local assistant front end**. Core2 already has the ingredients for interaction тАФ touch, mic, speaker, vibration, and battery-backed portability тАФ while the camera module and distance sensor add situational awareness. That means the MSI box can stay the тАЬbrain,тАЭ but the M5Stack hardware becomes the physical embodiment of the product: a push-to-talk controller, alarm/feedback device, and compact sensor bridge. The key design choice is to keep M5Stack focused on **structured signals and interaction**, not on heavyweight inference. ## Voice UX without breaking the local story

The right narrative for voice is **voice-first experience, local-first core**. On stage, the default path should stay fully local: local speech recognition, local reasoning, local speech output. That is technically realistic because whisper.cpp explicitly documents fully offline, on-device usage and broad platform support, and Piper is a fast local neural text-to-speech system. Combined with the eventтАЩs local-model recommendations around Ollama and Gemma, this gives PoliSa a complete offline voice loop that still feels polished. юИАciteюИВturn14view12юИВturn14view13юИВturn22view0юИВturn14view11юИБ

ElevenLabs should then be treated as a **backend-compatible enhancement layer**.
