\section{1. Architecture and product surface}

Houston is a local mission-control assistant for a Mars surface habitat. Mars is
the stress test where local AI is the correct architecture: Earth-Mars one-way
light time is 4--24 minutes and solar conjunction can produce multi-day
blackouts, so a cloud SLA cannot reach the operator in time.

\begin{tabularx}{\linewidth}{>{\bfseries}p{0.25\linewidth}X}
\toprule
Why Mars & It turns ``offline'' from a privacy claim into a physics requirement:
the cloud cannot answer during the operator's decision window.\\
Why small models & On an 18 GB M3 Pro, a fast 3B 4-bit model plus RAG, schemas
and streaming beat a larger model that wastes the latency budget.\\
Why citations & A habitat controller cannot hallucinate. Houston retrieves NASA
chunks first and either cites them or returns a deterministic fallback.\\
Why app integration & The useful product is not a chatbot: it ties local files,
PDF receipts, mic/TTS, sensor cache, 3D drill-ins and app state together.\\
\bottomrule
\end{tabularx}

\section{3. What we tried and learned}

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{MLX in-process is the operating point for this machine.} On the
  committed RAG-prefill benchmark, MLX Qwen2.5-3B hit \metric{57.6}{tok/s} and
  \metric{1,668}{ms TTFT}. The same plot keeps the slower local alternatives
  visible instead of hiding them.
  \item \textbf{Cache the workflow, not only the model.} Shared persona prefix
  gave a measured \metric{2.0x}{A2A speedup}; the Arrow/Parquet tile cache gave
  \metric{29.2x}{speedup} on time-window sensor queries.
  \item \textbf{Receipts matter on stage.} The PDF is not asking judges to
  trust us: every number below is regenerated from committed CSVs under
  \code{benchmarks/houston/out/light/}.
\end{itemize}

\section{4. Compliance and reproducibility}

At demo time Houston makes no hosted AI calls: no OpenAI, Anthropic, Google,
Groq, Together, Replicate, cloud embeddings, cloud ASR or cloud TTS. Runtime
services are local-only: Tauri app, FastAPI sidecar, MLX/Ollama, FAISS,
whisper.cpp and macOS \code{say}. We verified airplane mode with
\code{scripts/airplane-check.sh} and a 90-second \code{tcpdump} capture showing
zero outbound packets outside localhost. Reproduce with
\code{./scripts/setup.sh} \(\rightarrow\) \code{./scripts/download-mars-corpus.sh}
\(\rightarrow\) \code{LLM\_BACKEND=mlx npm run tauri:dev}.
